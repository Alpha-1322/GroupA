About Us

Our Vission and Ethos

At the london School of Science and Technology. We believe that education is not only about academic acheivement but is about the holistic individual. We want to ensure that students engage and enhance all aspects of their lives and in doing so help them to determine their own course, woth a good understanding of the world and the opportunities around them. The school will deliver this vision by focuscing on our four core valuse.


Aspiration


All Students should aspire to reach a particular destination in their lives. Our role at London School of Science ans Technology (LST) is to support students in ensuring that their aspiration is both ambitious and achievable and do our best to ensure our student reach that goal.


Integrity


We expect out student, staff and parents to act with integrity to all times. This means cautious and positive communication with each other, always being honest with themselves and others, and representing themselves, the School and community in a positive way


Exploration


Student should be curious with their learning with the use of new technology and want to explore beyound the subject matter they are taught in the Classroom. At LST, we hope to develop this value through our enrichment programed which will encourage student to try new things, through our trips and visits which require students to explore and aswer queations, and theough the challenging curriculum delivered by our superb staff.


Resilience


Resilience will be a core value at the London School of Science and Technology as we firmly believe that our young people need to be able to respond confidently to the changes or uncertainties they may face in their lives. We also know that resilient children are happier children. Through developing self-management skills,promoting a strong tradition and culture in the school, and broading knowledge as part of our curriculum, we can ensure that London School of Science and Technology students able to overcome any adversity.


A Unique Opportunity


Mile 91 is one of the fastest growing communities in Sierra Leone and yet lacks opportunities to provide quality education for our young generation. As a brand new School, we have choosen to do some things differently to other schools in the mile 91 community.
